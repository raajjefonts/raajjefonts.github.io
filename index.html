The Only Maldivian Fonts Repository You'll Need

Tip: press Ctrl + F to search fonts

RaajjeFonts brings together all free Dhivehi fonts for personal and commercial use in one place, making it easy for designers to discover and compare fonts across the web. When you click 'Download,' you'll be redirected to the original font source, whenever available.
